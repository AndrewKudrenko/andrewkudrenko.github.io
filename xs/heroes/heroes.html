XS HERO
AMIR KABBANI

* 04-07-1989 / Germany
Dirt Jump / Freeride Mountainbiker

YOU ONLY LIVE ONCE.
– AMIR KABBANI –

Amir started mountain biking at the age of 13. His active and creative lifestyle is mirrored in his sporting career. He loves being in the great outdoors, setting up his own spots and performing spectacular and unique tricks there. Since 2011, in the woods of his home town of Boppard, he has been organising the Fade Away Session, a dirt jump contest on self-built ramps. In 2013 he received the German web video prize for his video production "In the Woods". Further editions followed with "In the Woods 2 & 3". He was also awarded mountain biker of the year in 2009 and 2010.

XS HERO
MARIO ROWALD

* 04-07-1989 / Germany
Dirt Jump / Freeride Mountainbiker

THE REALITY FOLLOWS YOUR THOUGHTS.
– MARIO RODWALD –

Mario has been flying with his kite through the most exciting spots on earth since 2001. Since he won his first German junior championship title, he dominated German kitesurfing with eight further titles in the years 2007 - 2013. He is now also the undisputed number one in Europe – with three European championship titles in succession.
It is the power, the speed and at the same time the aesthetics that makes the sport so fascinating for him.
He is motivated by the positive vibe the sport gives him and by the opportunity to get to know various cultures all over the world through kitesurfing.

XS HERO
ALEX PASTOR

* 04-07-1989 / Germany
Dirt Jump / Freeride Mountainbiker

WHATEVER YOU DO IN LIFE HAS TO BE PASSION-DRIVEN.
– ALEX PASTOR –

At the age of eight Alex got his first windsurfing gear as a present by his father. In 2003 he stood on a kiteboard for the first time and couldn’t stop it. Only one year later he already spent every possible day kiting and entered his first competition, which he won right away. Alex Pastor loves to be at the beach and in the water at different spots all over the world, but in the end he always comes back to Tarifa (Spain) where it all started and where he is the owner of a kite school.
Since 2006 he competes in PKRA (Professional Kiteboard Riders Association) Tour Events, where he became Vice World Champion three years in a row until he finally achieved his biggest success so far: PKRA World Champion 2013.
